About SOK Kiwi Fruit Contracting

At SOK Kiwi Fruit Contracting, we specialise in providing reliable, efficient, and high-quality kiwifruit contracting services across New Zealand. With a passion for horticulture and a strong commitment to excellence, our team plays a key role in supporting growers throughout the entire kiwifruit production cycle.

Who We Are

Founded with hands-on experience and a deep understanding of the kiwifruit industry, SOK Kiwi Fruit Contracting has grown into a trusted name for orchard management and seasonal labour solutions. Our team consists of skilled supervisors and a well-trained workforce who are dedicated to helping growers achieve the best possible yield and fruit quality.

What We Do

We offer a full range of kiwifruit contracting services, including:

  • Winter Pruning
  • Summer Canopy Work
  • Thinning and Fruit Counting
  • Picking and Harvest Support
  • Vine Maintenance and Management
  • Qualified Supervision and Labour Supply

Whether you manage a small orchard or a large-scale operation, we tailor our services to suit your seasonal and long-term needs.

Our Mission

To deliver dependable, professional, and cost-effective kiwifruit contracting services that add real value to growers and the wider horticulture industry.
